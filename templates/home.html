TRADE MACHINE

How does it work?

1. Select the two teams you want to trade between

2. Click the type of trade you want.

a) Player Trade: A trade in which only players are traded for both teams. Atleast one player from each team must be selected.

b) Compensation Trade: A trade in which you choose a player from team one, and choose a salary that the second team pays. Multiple players can be selected.

3. For a trade to be valid, both teams' salary must be under 95 Cr (the salary cap) and the number of overseas players must be 8 or below after the trade

4. Once the trade is successful, you have the chance to submit the trade into the discussion forum. To do this, enter a title and a description for your discussion post

5. After you have finalized and submitted your trade, you have the ability to make playing elevens with both teams, with this trade included.

START TRADING!
